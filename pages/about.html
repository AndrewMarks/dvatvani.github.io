About

I’m Dinesh Vatvani, a 28 year old Data Scientist living in London. My acadmic background is in chemical informatics but I have since drifted into the world of Data Science.

This blog is an outlet for various bits of analysis I have worked on for my own interest. A lot of it will be related to the Quantified Self movement, since it’s a rich topic and one I’m highly interested in, but it may also include other topics depending on what sparks my interest at the time of writing the articles.

Most analysis and scripting will be done in python, as that’s my weapon of choice when facing analytcal problems.
